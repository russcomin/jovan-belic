My Story

Hy there! Im Jovan Belic, currently Lead Designer at Quarterback. I was born on 15th January 2001. Spent most of my days so far in Serbia, but i hope this will change in future because i would love to travel and learn from other people and nationalities. I started with Design&Development 2 years ago and my life really changed since then, actually it's changing on daily bases pretty much! I don't really love to spend a lot of my time in one place and im always trying to find a way to change something in my life. Im not afraid of making decisions, even if some of decisions are wrong they still keep me going and not staying in one place forver. Currently im really into Product Design and im having great time working with the team at QB. In my free time i like to work on side projects and on my skills in general, this is very important for self-growth!
