MY PROCESS
WHY & HOW

01

Sketching & Idea / Thinking

This is the best part of creating a digital product! Brainstorming, userstories, user journeys..
Everything from my head goes on paper, this is usually the longest step in creating a product.

Brainstorming

Brainstorming is really fun and non-linear process for me, im
trying to force myself to think about something from as much
perspectives as possible. Also im putting everything on paper,
so i can compare all solutions at the end when i need to choose only one.

Userstories

I really love to write/create userstories. I belive that by writing
userstories you force your brain to think about one thing from
different angles. I think im still learning this technique but anyway
im using it everyday and by that im trying to train my mind to do this
automaticlly

02

Wireframing & UX / Feeling

A bit more fun part but much more challenging. Now i have defined goal and idea and its time
to do something with that. In this step im making sure that my idea actaully make any sense.

Wireframing

I love wireframing. Im also doing a lot of wireframing
whenever im creating something. Good wireframe really
helps a lot.

User Interaction

Again im trying to think like a user. Where would i
click, what would i do... With that mindset im creating
User Interactions.

03

Lo-Fi & Hi-Fi / Doing

And of course here we are! Everything is ready to become alive, and i love to add life into everything. However this
is not my favorite part of the process, after so much work and love putted into project/product everything is comming to an end.

Low Fidelity UI

Lo-Fi for me is something like wireframe but in
digital form and a bit more detailed. And after
this step there is no going back.

High Fidelity UI

And finally we are here. By this time everything should
make sense, everything should work and look good.
I don’t really enjoy this step because its the last one.
